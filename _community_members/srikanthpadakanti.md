Srikanth Padakanti is a senior software engineer at Apple, where he builds large-scale data platforms and observability infrastructure. He actively contributes to multiple OpenSearch projects, including Data Prepper, OpenSearch core, SQL, and ML Commons. He holds a Master's degree in computer science from Texas A&M University.
